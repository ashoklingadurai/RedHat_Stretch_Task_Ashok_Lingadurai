How to Ride a Bike [Stretch Task]

Overview

This article covers the details on how to ride a motorcycle.

Explanation

  • Initially make sure the ignition is ON and the Fuel is switched ON

Note: It is always recommended to turn the fuel off while parking your bike during the end of the day.

  • After inserting the key, turn ON the ignition.

  • Make sure the side stand is taken off.

  • Start the vechicle by electric start (if applicable) or by kicker.

  • Leave the engine running for a couple of seconds to warm up the engine.

  • By holding the clutch, switch to first gear. Keep holding the clutch.

  • Slowly raise the acceleration as well as leave the clutch handle at the same time. Remember to do it slowly. Be patient if you are a first time user.

  • As you can see the bike starts to move. Once the bike reaches above 15kms (for 150cc vechile) switch to second gear by holding the clutch and repeat the same procedure till your bike moves in a nominal speed

Important Note

Always wear a helmet and drive responsibly.
